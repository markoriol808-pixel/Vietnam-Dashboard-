Vietnam Economic Dashboard (2021–2026)
This interactive dashboard provides an executive-level analysis of Vietnam’s economic trajectory over the last five years. Designed for Multi-National Corporations (MNCs) and strategic investors, it synthesizes macro-stability indicators, trade flows, and foreign direct investment (FDI) data into a single, interconnected view.

📈 Key Strategic Views
Macro Stability Pulse: Tracking the resilience of GDP growth vs. inflation to gauge market stability.

Top GDP Sectors: An interactive breakdown of the top 10 sectors, highlighting manufacturing and electronics as primary growth drivers.

Trade & Tariff Exposure: A dynamic view of export/import balances with an integrated Tariff Scenario Slider to model export value at risk.

FDI Attraction: Analyzing the gap between Registered and Disbursed capital to evaluate the actual "grounding" of foreign investments.
