Tariff Exposure and Site-Selection Risk

Executive Interpretation

Most attractive thesisVietnam combines high GDP growth, deepening electronics/manufacturing capacity, and strong FDI inflows. That supports a China+1 manufacturing case.
Primary risk thesisThe same export-led model that attracts manufacturers also creates tariff and U.S. demand exposure, especially in electronics, textiles, furniture, and footwear.
Recommended useUse Vietnam as a production and regional-platform candidate, but pair it with supplier localization, tariff classification review, and contingency planning for trade-policy shocks.
